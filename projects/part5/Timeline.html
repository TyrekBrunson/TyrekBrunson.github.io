50 Years of NSBE

2023

NSBE Innovators Summit 2023

The NSBE Innovators Summit 2023 brought together engineers, innovators, and industry leaders to explore the latest trends in technology and engineering. The event featured inspiring keynote speakers, hands-on workshops, and insightful panel discussions aimed at fostering the next generation of Black engineers. Attendees had the chance to network, exchange ideas, and leave with new knowledge and connections to propel their careers forward.

2022

NSBE Visionaries Conference 2022

The NSBE Visionaries Conference 2022 focused on future innovations and advancements in STEM. Engineers, students, and professionals gathered to share their experiences, participate in interactive sessions, and discuss ways to enhance diversity in the engineering industry. The event was an invaluable opportunity for attendees to learn about new technologies and establish meaningful connections within the engineering community.

2021

NSBE Pioneers Gathering 2021

The NSBE Pioneers Gathering 2021 celebrated the achievements of Black engineers and explored groundbreaking projects that had a lasting impact on the field. Through workshops, seminars, and networking opportunities, participants reflected on the progress made in STEM while pushing forward innovative ideas. The event left a mark on attendees, motivating them to continue driving positive change in both engineering and their communities.
