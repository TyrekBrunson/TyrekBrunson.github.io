Project Type: Main Characteristics: Benefits:
Crafting: Creative art projects using paper, paint, and recycled materials. Boosts creativity, improves fine motor skills, and provides family bonding time.
Home Decor: DIY decorations like wall art, personalized frames, or upcycled furniture. Adds a personal touch to home spaces and teaches basic design skills.
Gardening: Planting flowers, herbs, or vegetables in small backyard or indoor pots. Encourages outdoor activity, teaches responsibility, and connects with nature.
Woodworking: Building simple furniture, shelves, or garden boxes with wood. Teaches practical skills, promotes teamwork, and provides a sense of accomplishment.
Baking: Creating homemade treats like cookies, cakes, or bread with simple recipes. Enhances cooking skills, fosters creativity, and creates delicious memories.
