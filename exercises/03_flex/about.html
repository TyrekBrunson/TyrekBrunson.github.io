Interesting Facts

Did you know baby otters are called pups? They can stay with their parents for up to a year before they venture out on their own!

About Us

We are a group of otter enthusiasts dedicated to educating the world about these amazing creatures. Our mission is to provide accurate information and beautiful images to showcase the lives of otters, especially baby otters!

Our Mission

To spread awareness about the importance of otter conservation and the joy these creatures bring to their ecosystems.

Our Team

Our team consists of wildlife experts, photographers, and volunteers who all share a passion for otters.
